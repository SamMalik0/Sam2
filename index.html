A Living, Breathing Purifier

The AIROPOD isn't just a stylish home for your plant; it's an active air purification system. We've combined the natural, air-cleaning abilities of real plants (like the Peace Lily or Spider Plant) with smart, integrated technology.

It uses a whisper-quiet DC fan to actively draw polluted air through the plant's soil and extensive root system. This "bio-filtration" process allows natural microbes in the soil to absorb and break down harmful indoor gases (VOCs) far more efficiently than a plant can alone.

Help Section (FAQ)

How do I set up the pot?

It's simple! Pot your plant (we recommend a Peace Lily or Spider Plant) into the main compartment. Add water to the reservoir, plug in the power adapter, and it will start working immediately.

How do I replace the carbon filter?

For optimal performance, we recommend replacing the activated carbon layer every 6-8 months. The layer is easily accessible beneath the main potting chamber.
